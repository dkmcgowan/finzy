# Finzy 0.1.2

## Library Browse — Alpha Jump Bar

- **Fixed letter-to-scroll alignment**: Tapping a letter (e.g. H) now scrolls to the correct section instead of landing on a different letter (e.g. A).
- **Fixed scroll-to-letter display**: The highlighted letter in the jump bar now correctly reflects the first visible item.
- **Article stripping**: Titles starting with "The ", "A ", or "An " are normalized for grouping (e.g. "The Fabelmans" groups under F).
- **Row-start preference**: When jumping to a letter, the view scrolls so the target letter appears as the leftmost visible item when possible.

## Live TV

- **Channels tab layout**: Channel panels now match the size and spacing of the Programs tab for a consistent look across Live TV.

## Other

- Various fixes and improvements across authentication, i18n, screenshots, and video controls.
